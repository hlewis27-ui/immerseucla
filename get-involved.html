Fall 2026 Timeline

September
General Info Session

Learn about IMMERSE's mission, projects, and how to get involved. Meet our executive board and current members.

Early October
Membership Applications Release

Official applications open for all positions. Submit your application to join our executive board or general membership.

Mid-October
Join Your Desired Project

After acceptance, choose from our three main projects: Parkinson's VR, MotionSense Platform, or Becoming a Scientist VR.

October-November
Attend School Events

Participate in UCLA events, career fairs, and networking opportunities to build connections and gain exposure.

November-December
Demo Events

Showcase our projects at demo days, present research findings, and demonstrate VR simulations to the UCLA community.

December-Spring
Lead IRB Research Studies

Take leadership roles in IRB-approved research studies on our VR projects, contributing to medical education advancement.
