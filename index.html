About IMMERSE

IMMERSE is a UCLA student organization committed to fostering a deep engagement with the medical field through education, hands-on research experience, scientific exploration, and the cultivation of empathy in healthcare.

🎓

Medical Education

Explore medical concepts, clinical experiences, and pathways to healthcare careers through workshops and mentorship.

🔬

Research & Science

Engage in cutting-edge biomedical research projects and develop scientific literacy in modern medicine.

❤️

Empathy & Service

Build compassionate patient care skills and contribute to community health initiatives that make a real difference.

Our Core Pillars

1

Immersion

Deep engagement with medical topics, research, and healthcare environments to build practical knowledge and experience.

2

Education

Continuous learning through seminars, journal clubs, case studies, and expert guest speakers from the medical field.

3

Research

Original scientific inquiry and collaboration with faculty mentors on biomedical research initiatives.

4

Empathy

Developing patient-centered care perspectives and serving vulnerable populations in the Los Angeles community.

What We Offer

📚 Educational Workshops

Monthly seminars on medical topics, pre-med preparation, and healthcare career pathways.

🤝 Mentorship Program

Connect with medical students, physicians, and researchers for personalized guidance.

🧪 Research Opportunities

Participate in active research projects and publications with faculty collaborators.

🏥 Community Service

Volunteer with local healthcare organizations and community outreach initiatives.

🎤 Guest Speakers

Hear from leading physicians, researchers, and healthcare professionals.

🤝 Peer Network

Build lasting connections with like-minded pre-health and health-focused students.
