Project Overview

Becoming a Scientist is an immersive Virtual Reality educational simulation designed to democratize access to scientific research and laboratory techniques. Our mission is to make research more accessible for students at all education levels, from elementary school to graduate studies, by creating interactive experiences that bring real laboratory experiments into virtual environments.

Through partnerships with UCLA researchers and students, we showcase authentic laboratory techniques and experiments, making complex scientific concepts tangible and engaging. This project specifically targets underrepresented communities in Los Angeles, providing equitable access to STEM education and research experiences.

Featured Laboratory Experiments

Molecular Biology Techniques

Experience PCR amplification, gel electrophoresis, and DNA sequencing in a virtual lab setting. Learn about molecular cloning and genetic engineering techniques used in modern biomedical research.

Cell Culture & Microscopy

Master aseptic techniques, cell passaging, and microscopy. Explore cell biology concepts through interactive virtual experiments that demonstrate real laboratory protocols.

Neuroscience Research Methods

Learn about brain imaging techniques, electrophysiological recordings, and behavioral assays. Understand how neuroscience research contributes to our understanding of neurological disorders.

Data Analysis & Statistics

Practice statistical analysis of experimental data, learn about experimental design, and understand the importance of rigorous scientific methodology in research.

Technical Implementation

Development Platform: Unity Engine
Programming Language: C#
Hardware: Oculus VR Headset
Target Audience: Students from elementary to graduate level
Content Focus: Biomedical research techniques and scientific methodology

Development Timeline

July 2021
Project Initiation
Initial concept development and research into VR educational applications. Began partnerships with UCLA researchers to identify key laboratory techniques to showcase.
2022
Core Development
Built foundational VR environment and implemented first laboratory experiments. Focused on molecular biology and cell culture techniques.
2023
Content Expansion
Added neuroscience research methods and data analysis modules. Began community outreach programs in Los Angeles.
2024-Present
Ongoing Development
Continuous content updates, user feedback integration, and expansion to additional research techniques and educational modules.

Community Impact & Mission

Target Communities:

Underrepresented areas in Los Angeles

Becoming a Scientist is specifically designed to address educational inequities in STEM fields. By bringing advanced laboratory techniques to virtual reality, we provide students from underrepresented communities with access to experiences typically only available in well-funded research institutions.

Educational Impact

Democratizing Research

Making advanced scientific techniques accessible to students regardless of their school's resources or location.

Early STEM Engagement

Introducing younger students to research careers through engaging, interactive experiences.

Diverse Representation

Showcasing research from diverse UCLA scientists and students to inspire underrepresented groups.

Practical Skills

Teaching fundamental laboratory techniques that prepare students for real research opportunities.

Future Vision

Expanded Content: Addition of more research disciplines including environmental science, physics, and engineering
Collaborative Learning: Multi-user experiences allowing students to work together in virtual labs
Assessment Integration: Built-in progress tracking and educational assessment tools
Global Reach: Multilingual support and cultural adaptation for broader accessibility
Real-world Connections: Integration with actual UCLA research projects and mentorship programs
