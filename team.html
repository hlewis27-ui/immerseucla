Board Requirements & Term

All officers must be active members of IMMERSE and current UCLA undergraduate or graduate students. Officers serve for one academic year or until their successors are selected. Officers must maintain their status as UCLA students to remain in office.

Leadership Structure

The Executive Board is structured to ensure comprehensive oversight of all organizational functions and initiatives. With dedicated leadership in both internal operations and external engagement, IMMERSE maintains a balanced approach to achieving its mission.

Each board member brings unique perspectives and expertise, working collaboratively to advance medical education, research, and community service across UCLA and Los Angeles.

Our Board Members Are:
